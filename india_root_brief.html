What We've Built

A complete product — from first login to heir handover — working end-to-end on Android.

🔐 Authentication & Security Done

Full sign-up with Google, OTP verification, PIN-based vault encryption (Argon2 key derivation), biometric unlock, and auto-lock timer. The PIN never leaves the device.

Google OAuth OTP Biometrics Auto-lock
🏠 Asset Vault Done

Record and organise your family's financial assets. Home & Property is fully detailed. All other categories have structured forms ready for recording.

Property ★ Bank Accounts EPF / PF Gold Investments Insurance Vehicle
💬 Personal Messages Done

For each asset, leave a personal message for your family — in your own voice, face, or words. Audio recordings, video messages, and rich text, all encrypted locally.

🎙 Audio 🎥 Video ✍️ Text
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Management Done

Add family members — spouse, children, siblings — as nominees. Assign them to specific assets. Manage roles and control exactly what each person can see.

Add Members Assign to Assets Role Control
📦 India Root Package & Heir View Done

Share an encrypted package offline with nominees — they install the app, import the package with their PIN, and get a clean view of the family assets. Fully offline. No cloud needed.

Offline Share Import Flow Heir Dashboard
☁️ Backup & Restore Done

Back up your encrypted vault to Google Drive. Restore it on any new device after a phone change, reset, or loss. Your vault survives your device.

Google Drive Encrypted Backup Cross-device Restore
🏦 Multi-Vault Support Done

Manage multiple vaults in one app — your own assets and, separately, your in-laws' or parents' assets. Each vault is fully isolated with its own encryption and PIN.

Vault Switching Isolated Encryption Family Units
⚙️ Settings & Access Control Done

Access delay (time-lock before heir access), appearance, account management, privacy policy. Built for a polished, production-ready experience.

Access Delay Dark / Light Mode Change PIN

India's Vision

India Root is more than a consumer application. It is a critical component of Digital India designed to solve a trillion-dollar domestic crisis through sovereign, privacy-first technology.

🇮🇳

The "Ease of Succession"

Aligned perfectly with the Government's drive for "Ease of Living." Currently, post-loss paperwork is a traumatic administrative nightmare. India Root delivers undeniable relief and peace of mind to the Indian middle class by making family succession frictionless.

⚖️

Alleviating Judicial Burden

A massive proportion of the 4-5 crore pending cases in Indian courts stem from familial property and wealth disputes. By enforcing clear, structured asset mapping while the vault owner is alive, India Root pre-empts thousands of litigations.

🤝

The Digilocker Synergy

DigiLocker brilliantly secures government-issued credentials (Aadhaar, PAN). India Root perfectly complements this by securing private, undocumented family wealth (property locations, physical gold, crypto, heir instructions) — resolving the final missing piece of a citizen's digital life.

🛡️

Data Sovereignty (Atmanirbhar)

Built entirely in India, on a strict Zero-Knowledge Architecture. Indian citizens' most sensitive financial maps are never harvested, read, or monetized by corporations. Total privacy, total sovereignty, entirely on Indian soil.

India Root ensures this capital rapidly routes back to Indian families and into active domestic markets, directly fueling national growth.
